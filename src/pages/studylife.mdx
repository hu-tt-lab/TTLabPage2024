      #### 10 月～ 11 月

      - 工学部・情報エレクトロニクス学科・生体情報コースの 3 年生数名が、 **研究室に配属**されます。

      #### 12 月

      - 3 年生の**歓迎会**を行います。（忘年会も兼ねる）

      #### ～ 3 月

      - 配属されてから年度末までは、**英語論文ゼミ**へ参加したり、先輩方の**研究進捗報告会**へ参加し、
        翌年 4 月から始まる自分自身の研究テーマを模索し、その準備のための勉強などをします。
      - 2 月にある**先輩方の卒業論文発表会・修士論文発表会**を聴講すると、今後の良い指針となります。
      - 3 月に卒業される先輩方を見送る**追いコン**があります。
      - 学部卒業後に就職をねらう人は、この時期から就職活動をすることになります。
      - メンターとして, 研究室の先輩を１人以上選択してもらいます.
    </Ja>

    <En>
      #### October - November

      - Several 3rd-year students of the School of Engineering, Department of Information and Electronics, Course of Biomedical Information are **assigned to the laboratory**.

      #### December

      - A **welcome party** for 3rd-year students is held. (It also serves as a year-end party.)

      #### Until March

      - From the assignment to the end of the fiscal year, you will participate in the **English Paper Seminar** and attend the **research progress report meetings** of your seniors.
        You will explore your own research theme starting in April of the following year and study for its preparation.
      - Attending the **seniors' graduation thesis presentation and master's thesis presentation** in February will provide you with good guidelines for the future.
      - There is a **farewell party** for the seniors who graduate in March.
      - Those who aim to get a job after graduating from the undergraduate course will start job hunting from this period.
      - As a mentor, you will select one or more seniors in the laboratory.
    </En>

      #### 4 月

      - 舘野教授、西川准教授とよく話し合った上で、自分の**研究テーマを決定**します。
      - ここから、自分自身の研究が**本格的に**始まります。

      #### ～ 8 月

      - 研究テーマが決まってから夏ごろまでは、**進学希望者は研究と院試の勉強との両立**が問われます。
        就職希望者も研究と就職活動との両立を迫られます。がんばりどころです。
      - 7 ～ 8 月ごろ、数回に渡ってジンパ（ジンギスカン・パーティー）が催されることがあります。
      - 進学希望者は、8 月中旬ごろ、**大学院修士課程入学試験**に挑みます。

      #### 9 月～ 2 月

      - **自分の研究テーマに集中して取り組む時**です。非常に重要な期間です。
      - 12 月までには実験データをまとめ、**卒業論文の執筆**にかかります。
      - 1 月に**卒業論文タイトルの提出**があります。執筆に集中して取り組む時期です。
      - 2 月に**卒業論文の提出**と**卒業論文発表会**があります。

      #### 3 月

      - **卒業式・学位授与式**があります。**追いコン**で祝ってもらいます。
    </Ja>

    <En>
      #### April

      - After discussing with Professor Tateno and Associate Professor Nishikawa, you will decide on your **research theme**.
      - From here, your own research will start **in earnest**.

      #### Until August

      - From the time you decide on your research theme until around summer, **those who wish to advance to graduate school will be required to balance research and study for the entrance exam**.
        Those who wish to get a job will also be required to balance research and job hunting. This is the time to do your best.
      - Around July and August, there may be several Jingisukan (Genghis Khan) parties.
      - Those who wish to advance to graduate school will take the **entrance exam for the master's program of the graduate school** around mid-August.

      #### September - February

      - This is the time to **concentrate on your research theme**. It is a very important period.
      - By the end of December, you will compile experimental data and start **writing your graduation thesis**.
      - In January, you will submit the **title of your graduation thesis**. It is a time to concentrate on writing.
      - In February, there will be the **submission of your graduation thesis** and the **graduation thesis presentation**.

      #### March

      - There will be a **graduation ceremony and degree conferral ceremony**. You will be celebrated at the **farewell party**.

    <En>
      #### 1st Year in Doctoral Program, April

      - After discussing with Professor Tateno and Associate Professor Nishikawa, you will decide on your **research theme in the doctoral program**.
      - If there are people who have entered from other universities or other laboratories at Hokkaido University, we will hold a **welcome party for new members**.

      #### After That

      - You will conduct your research **under the guidance of your supervisor**.
      - You must **acquire the skills necessary to be a full-fledged researcher**, such as problem identification, research planning, experimental techniques, analytical techniques, and writing papers, and produce **results**.

      #### Until Degree Acquisition

      - After clearing the **requirements for a doctorate**, you will write your **doctoral thesis**.
      - Around November of the 3rd year, the administrative procedures for the degree examination will begin.
      - After passing the **preliminary examination and public examination**, you will obtain your degree by overcoming the defense.

      #### Last March

      - There will be a **graduation ceremony and degree conferral ceremony**. You will be celebrated at the **farewell party**.

## ゼミについて

### 進捗会

- 研究の進捗を報告し、先生方やラボ内の他メンバーからの助言を得る場。

### 英語論文ゼミ

- 各自の研究に関連する英語論文を読み、先生方やラボ内の他のメンバーに分かりやすく伝え、
  それについて議論するためのゼミ。
- この論文紹介は、博士後期課程、修士課程、学部 4 年(後期のみ）の学生が発表を行う。
- 月に一度程度、学部 3 年生～学部 4 年（前期）において、教員が用意した英語資料を読み、
  先生方やラボ内の他のメンバーに分かりやすく伝える発表を行う。
  （「科学技術英語」の授業として実施する。）

</Ja>

## Regular meetings in our lab

### Progress meetings

- A place to report the progress of your research and get advice from professors and other members in the lab.

### English paper seminars

- This is a seminar for students to read English papers related to their research, communicate them to professors and other members in the lab in an easy-to-understand manner, and discuss them.
  This is a seminar for students to read English papers related to their research, present them to the professors and other members in the lab in an easy-to-understand manner, and discuss them.

- Once a month, from the 3rd year undergraduate students to the 4th year undergraduate students (first semester), students read English materials prepared by the faculty and discuss them in an easy-to-understand manner with the professors and other members in the lab.
